How to Arrange Easy Hotel Pickups and Drop-offs

Hotel Transfers

A well-planned hotel pickup removes uncertainty and helps you stay on schedule for flights, meetings, dinners and local experiences. Planning the practical details before the journey can make the experience more comfortable and predictable.

Clear timing and an exact pickup point make hotel transfers effortless.

— Beyond Leisure Tours —

Confirm the correct entrance

Large hotels may have separate lobby, parking and conference entrances. Confirm the hotel name, address and preferred meeting point when making the booking.

Allow enough travel time

Cape Town traffic can vary by time of day. Build in additional time for airport journeys, appointments and evening events, especially during peak periods.

Share changes early

Inform the driver or booking team when your checkout, reservation or meeting time changes. Early communication helps protect the rest of the day's schedule.
