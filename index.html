About Beyond Leisure Tours

Discover Cape Town the way it is meant to be experienced. Beyond Leisure Tours provides comfortable private transfers and personalised travel services throughout Cape Town and the Western Cape.

Whether you are arriving at the airport, travelling to your hotel, attending an evening event or exploring the Cape Winelands, we provide a dependable and comfortable journey from pickup to destination.

Our Services

Our services include Cape Town airport transfers, hotel pickups and drop-offs, after-hours transportation, private Winelands travel and customised transport arrangements for individuals, couples, families and small groups.
