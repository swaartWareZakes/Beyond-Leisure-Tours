A First-time Visitor's Guide to Cape Town

Cape Town

Cape Town combines city life, mountains, coastline and wine country, so thoughtful planning helps first-time visitors make the most of it. Planning the practical details before the journey can make the experience more comfortable and predictable.

Cape Town is easier to enjoy when the journey between places is planned.

— Beyond Leisure Tours —

Group nearby attractions together

Plan city-centre and waterfront stops together, then reserve separate days for the peninsula or Winelands. This reduces unnecessary driving.

Keep weather alternatives

Mountain and coastal activities depend on conditions. Have alternative city or food experiences available when weather changes.

Use local transport knowledge

A local private driver can help with realistic journey times, pickup points and routes between the city's different areas.
